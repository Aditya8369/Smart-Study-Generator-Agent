Study Science

Evidence-Based Study Tips

Our AI is built on decades of cognitive science research for maximum retention.

1

Spaced Repetition

Reviewing material at increasing intervals boosts long-term retention by up to 200% compared to massed practice. Our flashcard system automates this for you.

2

Active Recall

Testing yourself forces your brain to actively retrieve information — strengthening neural pathways far more effectively than re-reading notes.

3

The Pomodoro Technique

25 minutes of focused study followed by a 5-minute break dramatically reduces mental fatigue and improves overall productivity throughout a session.

4

Interleaved Practice

Mixing different topics or problem types in a single session leads to better long-term learning than practicing one type repeatedly (blocked practice).

5

The Feynman Technique

Explain a concept in simple terms as if teaching it to a child. Gaps in your explanation reveal exactly what you still need to study.
